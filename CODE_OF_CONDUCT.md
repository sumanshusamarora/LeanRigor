# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

LeanRigor is committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or level of expertise.

## Expected behaviour

Community members should:

- be respectful and constructive;
- assume good intent while addressing impact honestly;
- focus criticism on ideas, code, and outcomes rather than people;
- share context and evidence when disagreeing;
- respect privacy, confidentiality, and security boundaries;
- help create an environment where newcomers can participate safely.

## Unacceptable behaviour

Unacceptable behaviour includes harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, sexualised language or attention, deliberate intimidation, doxxing, sustained disruption, and publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject comments, issues, pull requests, code, and other contributions that violate this code. Serious or repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent exclusion from project spaces.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the project maintainer through the contact method listed in [SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md). Do not use public issues for sensitive reports.

Reports will be reviewed as promptly and confidentially as practical. Retaliation against people who report concerns in good faith is not permitted.
